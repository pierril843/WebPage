About Me

My name is Robert R. Pierce. I am a Support Developer at QNX, working with many different types of embedded targets and technologies. Helping customers overcome their problems, I have gained broad knowledge on QNX IPC, BSPs and QNX libraries.

As a graduate of the Electronics Systems Engineering accredited engineering program provided by Conestoga College, I am prepared for the world of embedded engineering. With the unique background that my program offered, we learned the hardware and the software of embedded systems. My previous experiences include a large amount of project based work that allowed me to develop a vast number of skills, including Embedded C programming, Multithreaded programming, assembler and Linux system programming.

My hobbies outside of work include attending anime conventions, snowboarding in the winter, tinkering with embedded DIY project. I hope to added cosplayer to that list soon. This website, like many of my other projects, is here to help showcase my developing skills.
