About Me

My name is Robert R. Pierce I am a Support Developer at QNX, working with many different types of embedded targets. Helping customers overcome their problems I have gained extensive knowledge on QNX IPC, BSPs and QNX libraries. As a graduate of the Electronics Systems Engineering accredited engineering program provided by Conestoga College I am prepared for the world of embedded engineering. With the unique experience that my program offered, we learned the hardware and the software of embedded systems. A large amount of work was projects based and I found it gave me a vast number of skills including Embedded C programming, Multithreaded programming, assembler and Linux system programming. My hobbies outside of business include attending anime conventions, snowboarding in the winter, tinkering with embedded DIY project. Hope to added cosplayer to that list soon.
